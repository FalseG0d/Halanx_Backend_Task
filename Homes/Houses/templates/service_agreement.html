1. TERM

The term of this Agreement shall commence from {{ house.agreement_commencement_date | date:'d M, Y' }} ("Effective Date") and shall remain in full force and effect until either Party terminates the Agreement ("Term"). In the event that either Party desires to terminate the Agreement during the Term, the Agreement shall be terminated in accordance with Clause 10 of this Agreement.

2. APPOINTMENT AND EXCLUSIVITY OF ENGAGEMENT

The Owner hereby appoints and engages Halanx Homes on an exclusive basis during the Term of this Agreement to provide residential services with respect to the Scheduled Property. The Parties agree that Halanx Homes shall be the sole source of, agreement to any license of the Scheduled Property, whether such agreement is made in writing or orally, though Owner can engage other sources during notice period.

3. LICENSING OF SAID PREMISES

Halanx Homes and Owner shall partner to give the Scheduled Property on license to the verified Licensee as per the following process:

  1. Owner shall specify his Licensee preference and verification requirements.
  2. Owner shall specify the monthly License Fee expected from Licensee
  3. Owner & Halanx Homes shall execute this service agreement.
  4. Halanx Homes shall onboard the house on its website including Licensee preference, monthly License Fee, list individual rooms at a rate including its charges.
  5. Halanx Homes shall show the Said Premises to interested parties & if any party expresses interest, Halanx Homes shall collect verification details from the Licensee.
  6. The Owner authorizes Halanx Homes to negotiate and finalize the monthly License Fee
  7. Halanx Homes shall execute the Leave and License agreement with Tenant on behalf of owner & complete other move-in formalities on behalf of Owner.
  8. During the term of the Leave and License Agreement, Owner has authorized Halanx Homes to collect the monthly License Fee from tenants and deposit it in his/her account after deducting its charges. Halanx Homes acts as a facilitator and is not the direct licensee to the Owner. Halanx Homes shall make a separate payout to the Owner in case the Licensee defaults on payment of the License Fee.
  9. The Owner shall not change the License Fee before end of eleven months from the date of move-in of the Licensee if the Licensee is still staying in the Scheduled Property
  10. Halanx Homes shall facilitate maintenance work required at the Scheduled Property, with actual charges paid by Owner or Tenant as per Clause 8.
  11. Move-in Date shall mean the date on which the Licensee physically intends to/actually occupies the Scheduled Property

4. SCOPE OF SERVICES

4.1 Halanx Homes shall provide the owner with services as mentioned in Annexure A of this Agreement ("Services") to enable licensing of the Scheduled Property to suitable Licensee. Such Services will start from Effective Date.

4.2 The owner hereby grants authority to Halanx Homes to perform all actions necessary to discharge services to the Owner, Scheduled Property & tenants as detailed in Annexure A. Any such actions shall be deemed ratified & approved by the owner. Additionally, the Owner grants Halanx Homes the right to enter the Scheduled Property at anytime during the term hereof.

5. LICENSEE VERIFICATION

5.1. For verification, Halanx Homes will mandatorily collect ID proof & address Proof of Licensee. It shall further collect Licensee's employment proof, wherever available & permanent address details.

5.2. Halanx Homes shall also assist Owner for the verification of the Licensee through police department of the concerned state where the Licensee resides.

5.3. Once a Licensee expresses interest to take on License the Scheduled Property, all these verification details shall be submitted to the Owner for approval.

5.4. The owner shall have a 48-hour window to approve or reject the tenant, failing which the Licensee shall be considered to have been approved by the Owner. The Licensee can move-in to the property only after Owner's approval.

5.5. In case of rejection, the Owner shall specify the reason, which shall be communicated to the interested party & Halanx Homes shall continue showing the Scheduled Property to other prospects

6. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Owner authorizes Halanx Homes to keep all or part of the security deposit of the scheduled property, which will be refunded to tenant as per the clauses, after deduction of any dues, if any.

7. LICENSE FEE

7.1. The Owner, in discussion with Halanx Homes, shall have the absolute authority to decide the monthly License Fee per Licensee and minimum lock-in period for the Licensee, which shall not exceed 6 months from the date from which the Licensee is liable to pay License Fee for the Scheduled Property.

7.2. The Owner shall be entitled to change the License fee before the tenant moves in. Thereafter, he shall change the License fee based on mutual discussion with the tenant at the end of every lease period.

7.3. For any given month, Halanx Homes shall collect the License Fee from the Licensee(s) & deposit it in Owner's virtual Halanx wallet before 3rd of the next month after deducting its charges & any other amounts payable by the Owner to Halanx Homes. And that amount in wallet can be transferred to owner's bank account whenever he or she requests. For example â€“ rent for the month of April shall be paid to the Owner on or before 3rd May.

7.4. In case of default in payment by a Licensee in any given month, Halanx Homes shall remind the tenant via email on daily basis for the payment till the end of the coming month. If it doesn't receive the License Fee within a month of its becoming due, Halanx Homes, on Owner's behalf, shall initiate legal proceeding against the defaulter tenant asking them to pay the due & vacate the property at the earliest.

7.5. If Halanx Homes determines that the owner is owed License Fee money from tenants for more than 30 days, with necessary consent from owner, reduce the security deposit payout to the tenant and ask the tenant to move out.

7.6. To allow sufficient time for rent collection in case of late payment/default, Halanx Homes shall have the option to collect License Fee from Licensees at the beginning of month.

7.7. For purposes of clarity, it is expressly agreed that the Owner's rental income is based on actual occupancy at the property. This Agreement creates no liability on Halanx Homes for rental payment during the period the property remains fully or partially vacant.

7.8. It is expressly agreed that all payments shall be made through bank accounts/Account Payee Cheques only.

8. OTHER PAYMENTS

The Said Premises, fittings provided in the Said Premises as well as the furnishings & appliances etc, if provided by Owner in Said Premises shall require maintenance/ repair at different times during the period of this agreement. The same shall be settled in following way

8.1. Maintenance of Said Premises:

8.1.1. The following list has been prepared in compliance with Model Residential Tenancy Act 2011, a draft by Ministry of Housing & Urban poverty alleviation, Govt. of India. As per this draft Act, the owner will be responsible for repairs relating to matters falling as under:

  1. Structural Repairs except those necessitated by the damage caused by the tenant.
  2. Whitewashing of walls and painting of doors and windows. The owner shall paint the interior, doors & windows at the beginning of the agreement period & thereafter Halanx Homes shall ensure the same after minimum service agreement tenure of 3 years.
  3. Changing Internal plumbing pipes when necessary
  4. Any type of electricity & water supply problem arising from outside the premises like voltage fluctuation.

8.1.2. While taking handover of the Said Premises from the owner, fresh painting and cleaning of the Said Premises shall be done by the Owner. Halanx Homes shall inspect the Said Premises and any repair work that might be required as communicated to the owner within 2 days from handover of property shall be addressed and borne by the Owner.

8.1.3. Any repair, replacement & maintenance issues in the Said Premises arising during the first 15 days of the moving in of the first tenant, will be born by owner. Thereafter, the charges for such maintenance shall be borne by tenants. Any cost incurred in relation to the above shall be settled in the following manner:

  1. Time
    Beginning of Agreement at the time of handover
  2. Expense Nature
    Payment, if any, made by Halanx Homes on behalf of owner to tenants/vendor/Society Maintenance etc. any other person for structural repairs, wear & tear, cleaning of house etc.
  3. Settlement Process
    Halanx Homes shall submit necessary bills, estimates & invoices & seek owner's approval over email. The amount paid by Halanx Homes shall be adjusted against payable monthly rent.
  4. Time
    During Lease Period
  5. Expense Nature

    Amount less than Rs. 500 towards structural repair

    Halanx Homes shall submit necessary bills, estimates & invoices & seek owner's approval over email. Owner shall approve or reject within 24 hours or it will be considered auto-approved


    Amount more than Rs. 500 towards structural repairs.

    Halanx Homes shall submit necessary bills, estimates & invoices & seek owner's approval over email. Owner shall approve or reject within 48 hours or it will be considered auto-approved


    Pending utility bills & other statutory payments including society maintenance fees

    Halanx Homes is authorized to pay upto INR 5000 & reimburse the same from owner's rent without having to wait for owner's approval if it doesn't come within 24 hours Explicit Owner approval shall be required for payment above Rs. 5000.


    The owner shall be able to dispute a payment within 48 hours of such a payment being made on his behalf with necessary documentation including original bills & receipts. If Halanx Homes, based on the documents submitted, determines that the owner is owed money, it shall send a refund payout to the owner's wallet within next 48 hours.

8.2. MAINTENANCE OF FIXTURES, FURNISHING & APPLIANCES PROVIDED AT THE PROPERTY BY OWNER

8.2.1. Halanx Homes shall provide a fixture checklist to the Owner at start of the Agreement based on which the Owner shall make the necessary arrangements. This checklist shall detail the repair & replacement of existing fitting requirement before onboarding the Said Premises on Halanx Homes Network. Halanx Homes, at the Owner's option, shall facilitate completing the items as per checklist at the property through its partner vendors.

8.2.2. The cost of maintaining the furniture and fixture shall be divided as under:

  • Paid by Tenant Day to day maintenance including:
    1. Changing of tap washers and taps, Drain cleaning, Water closet repairs, Wash Basin repairs
    2. Circuit breaker repairs, Switches and socket repairs, Replacement of bulbs, Repairs and replacement of electrical equipment except major internal and external wiring change
    3. Periodic maintenance of private gardens and open spaces, if any, let out to the tenant.
  • Paid by Owner Required periodic servicing and breakdown maintenance on account of age of the appliances, major manufacturer fault, power supply fluctuation or out of warranty (and need replacement) during the License Period of the all electronic appliances and furnishing provided by the Owners. However, any breakdown maintenance, on account of damage/unintended/wrong use by tenants shall be borne by tenants.

8.2.3. Owner owned fixtures & furnishings shall be subject to wear & tear depreciation as per Indian GAAP ("Generally Accepted Accounting principles") to ascertain their value in case of a damage claim. The Owner shall store & furnish receipts, bills, invoices & warranty cards, if any, of furnishing & fittings as & when a damage claim is filed for faster settlement.

8.3. The Owner shall respond to any maintenance request approval within 48 hours. In case Halanx Homes pays any maintenance charges on behalf of the Owner, such payments shall be governed under "Other Payments" section of this agreement.

8.4. In case damages to the property or its furnishing are caused by tenants, the Owner / Halanx Homes shall be entitled to recover damage charges from the security deposit. This is to clarify that Halanx Homes shall be liable to repair at the scheduled premise including electric, plumbing, furniture & appliance repair. Parts, consumables & service charge incurred during service visit shall be borne by the tenants.
